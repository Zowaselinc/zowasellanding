MARKETPLACE PLATFORM
Matchmaking and transaction sercurity.

We match buyers and sellers of the same commodity. Our platform connects thousands of crop sellers, with leading crop buyers locally and internationally. We are enabling buyers and sellers of crops to join our Preferred Network to securely trade with a trusted counterparty. Buyers and sellers can post, receive bids, negotiate, match, haul, and get paid at the click of a button directly with one another. You decide on pricing and terms together, Zowasel enforces smart contracts and ensures that all transactions are shipped and payments are made after delivery without any issues.
